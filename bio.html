Who is Rumaysa?

I'm a UI/UX design student at the University of Melbourne with a deep passion for creating intuitive and engaging user experiences. My curiosity across a wide range of topics has sharpened my ability to design with empathy and attention to detail.

With a strong foundation in design principles, I strive to craft experiences that are not only functional but also emotionally resonant. I believe that good design goes beyond aesthetics — it’s about understanding users, addressing their needs, and solving real problems in thoughtful ways.

My goal is to design interfaces that not only look great but also improve usability, accessibility, and connection between people and technology.

Feel free check out my portfolio for more of my work!
